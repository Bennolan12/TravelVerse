Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Visit the iconic Eiffel Tower, explore world-class museums, and stroll along the Seine River.

  • Best Time: April - May, September - October
  • Must-See: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame
  • Activities: Sightseeing, Dining, Shopping, Museums

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern innovation. Experience serene temples, bustling markets, cutting-edge technology, and exquisite cuisine.

  • Best Time: March - May, September - November
  • Must-See: Senso-ji Temple, Shibuya Crossing, Imperial Palace
  • Activities: Temple Visits, Shopping, Dining, Tech Exploration

New York, USA

The Big Apple never sleeps. From Broadway shows to world-famous museums, iconic skyscrapers to Central Park, New York offers endless entertainment and experiences.

  • Best Time: May - June, September - October
  • Must-See: Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park, Empire State Building
  • Activities: Theater, Museums, Dining, Shopping

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, ancient temples, vibrant culture, and lush rice terraces. Perfect for relaxation and adventure.

  • Best Time: April - October (dry season)
  • Must-See: Tanah Lot Temple, Ubud, Mount Batur, Rice Terraces
  • Activities: Beach Relaxation, Surfing, Hiking, Cultural Tours
